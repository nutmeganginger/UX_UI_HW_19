MY UX SKILLS

User Research

It is necessary to use both quantitative and qualitative research to get a full view of your user. Surveys provide a baseline and interviews let you explore more.

Competitor Analysis/SWOT

Defining how competitors stand out will help inform what you need to do to differentiate and cater to the users better. It is extremely beneficial to inventory your own strengths and weaknesses as well.

Prototyping

I excel in seeing the vision of a product from the research and designing it out. My attention to detail makes prototyping come naturally to me. I love seeing a product come to life.
